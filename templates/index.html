About This Application

This application allows you to analyze electrochemical test data from coin cells by generating various plots:

  • Charge-Discharge Curves: Voltage vs. capacity profiles for different cycles
  • Capacity vs. Cycle: Track capacity retention over cycling
  • State of Health: Visualize battery health degradation
  • Coulombic Efficiency: Analyze charge/discharge efficiency
  • Voltage vs. Time: Examine voltage variations over time
  • Differential Capacity: Identify phase transitions (dQ/dV)
  • Combined Performance: Comprehensive visualization of battery performance
